Orbital Intelligence,
Built for Precision

Every module of SpaceShield AI is designed around one goal: give you an unflinching picture of what's in orbit, how fast it's moving, and whether it's a threat.

🛰️

AI Debris Scanner

Upload any orbital image and Llama 3.2 Vision instantly classifies the object — spent rocket stages, solar panel fragments, bolt shards — with confidence scoring and threat classification.

Computer Vision Pipeline

Roboflow-backed deep learning model trained specifically on space debris imagery. Detects, classifies, and measures objects with bounding-box precision — even at confidence thresholds as low as 15%.

What's Coming to SpaceShield AI

The mission doesn't stop here. We're building the next generation of orbital intelligence tools — from predictive collision paths to satellite constellation health monitoring.

🔮

Collision Prediction Engine

ML-driven conjunction analysis that forecasts close-approach events up to 72 hours ahead, with probability curves and recommended avoidance windows.

🗺️

TLE Orbit Propagator

Input any Two-Line Element set and watch the real orbital path projected in 3D — past, present, and 24-hour future — rendered live in the browser.

📸

Multi-Frame Video Scanner

Upload a video clip from telescopic or satellite cameras. The system scans frame-by-frame, tracks debris motion, and produces a consolidated threat report.

🛡️

Satellite Health Dashboard

Monitor active satellite fleets — power, attitude, communication status, debris proximity buffer — from a single unified operations panel.

🤖

Autonomous Alert Agent

An always-on background agent that watches designated orbital regions and sends SMS or email alerts the moment a new high-risk object is detected.

📊

Debris Density Heatmaps

Time-animated global heatmaps showing how debris density evolves across orbital shells over days, months, and years — critical for mission planning.
